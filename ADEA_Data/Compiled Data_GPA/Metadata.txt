This data is prepared by Xianbin Xu for 50_dental projects. It included GPA for enrollees and applicants from 2000 to 2021.
The "type" column tells the origin of the data, be it "applicant", "First Time First Year Applicant", or "First Year Applicant".

School-Level data are kept in each school, and usually not released (or not released back to 2000).

The data are available in CSV and DTA format.
